About


Grand Finale Space Park is an amusement park located, as the name suggests, in space. It orbits the Earth fully on a daily basis, yet we have takeoff points in -

  • Paris, France
  • London, England
  • Berlin, Germany
  • Moscow, Russia
  • Beijing, China
  • Shanghai, China
  • Seattle, US
  • Los Angels, US
  • Orlando, US
  • New York City, US
  • St. Louis, US
  • Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
  • Sydney, Australia
  • Seoul, South Korea
  • Tokyo, Japan
  • Houston, US

And we still have even more on the way!
